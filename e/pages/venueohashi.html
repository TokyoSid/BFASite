GROUND & FOOTWARE

The surface is made of brand new, 3rd generation astroturf, which is great for playing on. The long bladed turf will protect children from injury should they fall over during practice and we reccomend rubber soled turf shoes for all players (please see our Footware Chart).
* Parents: please do not wear heeled shoes, take prams/pushchairs or food onto the astroturf.

GETTING THERE

By Car
There is parking available to the public in a paid carpark inside the junction right next to the pitch, accessible from the small road next to the river. DO NOT PARK INFRONT OF THE GATES NEXT TO THE PITCH - THIS IS AN EMERGENCY HIGHWAY ENTRANCE!

By Train
Ohashi Junction is located close to the East exit of Ikejiri Ohashi Station on the Hanzomon/Shin-Tamagawa line (5 min walk), this is just 1 stop (2 mins) from Shibuya.

By Taxi
Ask the driver to go to this address: Meguro Ohashi Yubinkyoku(post office) it is then just along the small street next to the river!

RAIN PLAN:

In the event of rain, sessions can go ahead. If there is heavy rain, snow (thick on the ground), lightning, or the ground is in poor condition due to earlier inclement weather the ground must not be used, and the sessions will be cancelled.

Please understand that there will be no refund or make up sessions in the event of cancelled sessions due to the weather/ground condition. We will inform* you by 9:30 am** if the sessions will be cancelled via our Facebook page

*Note: We will inform you only in case of doubtful/inclement weather. Please also understand that we might have to cancel after 9:30 am if the weather/ground condition worsens after 9:30 am.

VENUE RULES:

Please also make sure you read and comply the regulations in the BFA members guide.

Rules @ Ohashi Junction Futsal Court

  • In order for everybody to use the OPASU YUME HIROBA artificial grass ground safely, comfortably and for a long time, please comply with the following rules.
  • Important: Please make sure you bring the correct footwear. NO spikes, cleats or studs (either moulded or metal). Please bring futsal shoes or other flat soled shoes.
  • You cannot bring food or drink onto the artificial pitch. If you want to eat or drink you can do so in the areas outside of the pitch.
  • There are no rubbish bins at this facility. Please make sure you take away ALL your rubbish (magazines, newspapers, pet bottles, coffee cups, food wrappers etc) with you.
  • Pets are not allowed on the playing surface. Please walk your pet in the areas outside of the artificial grass.
  • Adults and children can play together on the pitch, so please be careful to avoid any accidents.
  • In case of serious accident or injuries, please contact the TENKUU TEIEN management office or 119 immediately.
  • To protect the artificial grass surface, please refrain from wearing high heels on it or riding bicycles, kick-boards, skate boards, baby buggies etc. on it.
